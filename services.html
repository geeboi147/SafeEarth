Our Services

Comprehensive environmental, hygiene, and facility management solutions.

Pest Control & Fumigation

Professional eradication of rodents, insects, and other pests. We ensure your premises are safe from disease-carrying organisms using WHO-approved methodologies.

Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

A sustainable approach to managing pests by combining biological, cultural, physical, and chemical tools in a way that minimizes economic, health, and environmental risks.

Environmental Health

Monitoring and controlling environmental factors that impact public health, ensuring the air, water, and immediate environments are safe for human occupation.

Cleaning & Sanitation

Deep cleaning, routine janitorial services, and specialized industrial sanitation for offices, factories, hospitals, and residential complexes.

Waste Management

Professional collection, transportation, disposal, and recycling of solid, liquid, medical, and hazardous wastes in full regulatory compliance.

Sewage & Drainage

Sewage evacuation, septic tank maintenance, blocked drain clearing, and comprehensive effluent management services.

Disinfection & Decontamination

Rapid response bio-hazard and viral disinfection services (including COVID-19 protocols) using medical-grade antimicrobial formulations.

Food Safety Support

Assisting food manufacturing and processing businesses to maintain strict hygiene standards in line with HACCP and ISO 22000 requirements.

HSE Consultancy

Expert Health, Safety, and Environment advice, risk assessments, safety audits, and development of robust HSE management systems.

Professional Training

Delivering certified courses in general health and safety, first aid, fire safety, and environmental awareness for corporate teams.

PPE Supply

Sourcing and distribution of high-quality Personal Protective Equipment, including coveralls, boots, masks, and customized safety gear.

Facility Management

End-to-end management of your physical assets, ensuring your buildings and infrastructure are optimally maintained for functionality, comfort, and safety.
