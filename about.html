About SafeEarth

Dedicated to protecting health, preserving environments, and ensuring safety.

Our Story

SafeEarth Environmental Services Ltd was founded on the principle that every home, workplace, and public space deserves to be clean, safe, and fully compliant with health regulations. Over the years, we have grown into a leading provider of integrated environmental and facility solutions in Nigeria.

We combine deep industry expertise, highly trained personnel, and modern technology to deliver solutions that span pest control, waste management, deep cleaning, and health & safety consultancy.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of integrated facility, environmental, hygiene and safety solutions, setting the benchmark for excellence across the continent.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, safe and effective environmental services that protect health, improve quality of life, and elevate organizational standards.
